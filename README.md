# Open Mastery Standard

An open conceptual framework for recognizing genuine understanding.

## What This Is

The Open Mastery Standard defines what it means to genuinely understand something. It establishes the conceptual criteria for recognizing mastery and the epistemic principles that govern valid claims of understanding.

It is not an assessment instrument. It does not prescribe specific tasks, scoring rubrics, or classroom procedures. It defines the conditions that any evidence of mastery must satisfy — regardless of how that evidence is gathered or evaluated.

**The core definition:**
> Mastery is the sustained coherence of understanding derived from mechanism, demonstrated through application to novel contexts, and confirmed by stability across variation and time.

Four conditions must all be met: **mechanism**, **novel application**, **stability**, and **coherence**.
